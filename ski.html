Golf Course Sking beginners/ Basic Learning

Welcome to Gulmarg. Here we have snow clad peaks, mountains,
slopes altogether open from December to April, offering ski
rental, heli- skiing, high altitude skiing, touring, powder
skiing, ice skating among others for everyone. Whether you are a
beginner or an extreme skier you have got the right place.
We also have excellent facilities for cross-country skiing,
sport tours and extreme sport experiences. If you want to ski down
Mountbr Affarwat,ski in the Backcountry, ski without any trees or traverse the
mighty Alpines, we offer you the right topography to quench
your thirst for winter adventures. The terrain of Gulmarg recieves
enough snowfall for anyone to shout powwww. Fair enough
but one needs to know the navigable/skiable areas of this vast
topography. Our guides are the best bet for a sound and safe experience.

TRIP DETAILS

The primary objective is arrrival in Srinagar, followed by lunch
in Srinagar itself or in Tangmarg (depending on client's wish).

ITINERARY

What follows is a general flow of events. Expect the unexpected and
prepare to be flexible.

DAY 1

Change of transport (taxi/cab etc) in Tangmarg. The change is due to the
fact that vehicles from here are only allowed if they are apt
for driving through deep snow conditions. So appropriate transport facility
will be provided from here till Gulmarg. On arrival to the destination
i.e. Gulmarg, clients will be taken to their respective pre-booked hotels/lodges/huts etc.
After a brief resting period of 2-3 hours the client will be accompanied by our team member
for hiring the appropriate skiing/snowboarding kit. This will be followed by a brief instruction/lecture
by an experienced team member about how to carry
on with the course/trip etc.

DAY 2

The next day we start at 9:00 in the morning sharp. Followed by a little briefing
and warm-up, the team will head towards the above mentioned
skiing/sowboarding slopes. The 1st half carries till 1300 hrs.
Followed by lunch. The post lunch session will further the skking/snowboarding experience, till we
conclude the day at 1600 hrs. The group will return to their
lodging place and rest.

DAY 3,4,5

On the coming days we will continue with the skiing/boarding experience, changing
slopes one after the other depending on the clients wish but
also depending on how one handles the
experience/practice/trainings.

DAY 6

The 6th day we have to report at Gulmarg Gondola Base Car Station at
10 o'clock in the morning. After collection of tickets the Gondola will ferry
the group/indivisual up to Kongdoori
(Base camp for high altitude adventures).
In the 1st session one can ski in the soft fluffy powder snow in presence
of the trainer. Beginners are'nt allowed to ski/snowboard the higher
verticals so for that reason the flat horizontals will
be preffered for this particular trip/course.
The 2nd session is geared towards sight seeing and various other
activities like Snow Bike Riding, Sledging, Toboganning, Snow Cycling, Photography
etc. This will conclude the day.

DAY 7

The last day which is the departure, will commense according to your
flight timings. We preffer to start early as one can expect traffic jams on
the way to the airport sometimes. Wishing you a
happy and safe journey.
